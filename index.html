Origins of AOT!

Attack on Titan is a Japanese action, dark mystery manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. It started airing on April 7th, 2013, and is one of the most popular manga series in Japan and one of the most anticapted anime this year as season four is continuing to air every Sunday since December 7th. The plot is centered around people living inside walls for as long as they can remember, fending off giant beasts called titans in order to survive. However, the plot only gets more complex as the episodes progress and by season four, it progresses to encompass ideas of morality, right and wrong, discrimination, etc.

Why I Chose AOT Out Of Everything ^-^

I started watching "Attack On Titan" around 2017 and since then, it's kind of been a comfort show. I'm attached to it and to all the characters and love it with all my heart. The plot and its complexities are so interesting and also the strong female characters make it better :D!! (All the characters are well written too.) The concept alone is great as it is and it allows for more intricacies as the plot deepens.
